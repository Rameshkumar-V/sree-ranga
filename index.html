About Sri Ranga Fine Arts

Sri Ranga Academy of Fine Arts, founded in 2009 by Shri K. Rajadurai Krishnamoorthi, is a proud initiative dedicated to preserving and promoting the timeless beauty of Indian classical arts in rural communities. With over a decade of excellence, the academy offers expert training in Bharatanatyam, Carnatic Vocal Music, Violin, Veena (Veenai), Drawing, and more ‚Äî guided by experienced and passionate gurus.

Our Mission: To inspire and educate the next generation in classical dance, music, and fine arts by offering authentic, accessible, and disciplined training.

Our Vision: To become a center of excellence in Indian fine arts, empowering rural talents with opportunities to embrace and excel in traditional art forms.

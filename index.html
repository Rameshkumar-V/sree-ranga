About Sri Ranga Fine Arts

Sri Ranga Academy of Fine Arts is a dedicated initiative committed to promoting the rich heritage of Indian classical arts in rural communities. Since its inception, the academy has been a nurturing space for aspiring artists, offering structured training in Bharatanatyam, Carnatic Vocal Music, Violin, Veena (Veenai), Drawing, and more — all under the guidance of passionate and experienced gurus. We believe in learning through practice, which is why our students are given maximum opportunities to perform on stage, participate in events, and grow their confidence as artists. Our mission is to make classical arts accessible, inspiring, and lifelong.

Our Mission: To inspire and educate the next generation in classical dance and music by providing authentic and structured training.

Our Vision: To be a beacon of tradition, creativity, and discipline — enabling students to embrace their artistic potential.
